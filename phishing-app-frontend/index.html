How the AI Works

Our phishing detector uses rule-based heuristics and lexical analysis—no external AI APIs.

URL Structure

We inspect domain length, subdomains, and suspicious patterns (e.g. IP addresses, unusual TLDs, homograph-style characters).

Heuristic Rules

Predefined rules flag common phishing signals: login/keyword stuffing, brand mimicry, and known malicious URL traits.

Risk Score

A 0–100 score is computed from these features. Lower means safer; higher means more indicators of phishing.

Your Privacy

URLs are analyzed on our server for scoring only. We don’t store or log your scans on the backend; history is local to your device.
